Key Governance Principles

🛡️
Duty of Care
Committee members must act with the care that an ordinarily prudent person would exercise in similar circumstances.
⚖️
Duty of Loyalty
Place the organization's interests above personal interests and avoid conflicts of interest.
📜
Duty of Obedience
Ensure compliance with laws, regulations, and the organization's governing documents.
