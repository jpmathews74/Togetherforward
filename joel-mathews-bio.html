Joel Mathews brings over 10 years of comprehensive experience in investment management, nonprofit finance, and strategic advisory services to his role as Chief Executive Officer of Together Forward Capital. His extensive background combines technical proficiency with a genuine commitment to helping organizations achieve their missions through sound financial stewardship.

Professional Experience

Joel's career spans multiple facets of the financial services industry, with particular expertise in serving nonprofit organizations, foundations, and high-net-worth individuals. Prior to leading Together Forward Capital, he held senior positions including CFO and CCO roles at financial services firms, where he developed and implemented investment strategies for institutional clients.

Throughout his career, Joel has demonstrated exceptional leadership in building and managing investment advisory practices. His experience includes establishing and overseeing compliance frameworks, developing client investment strategies, and guiding organizations through complex financial decisions. He has successfully navigated clients through various market cycles while maintaining a focus on long-term value creation and risk management.

Areas of Expertise

  • Nonprofit Investment Management: Specialized knowledge in endowment and foundation portfolio management, including UPMIFA compliance and fiduciary best practices
  • Strategic Financial Planning: Comprehensive experience in developing investment policy statements, asset allocation strategies, and spending policies aligned with organizational missions
  • Regulatory Compliance: Deep understanding of SEC regulations, FINRA requirements, and nonprofit investment regulations
  • Wealth Management: Expertise in serving high-net-worth individuals and families, including trust services and estate planning considerations
  • Alternative Investments: Knowledge of private securities offerings and alternative investment strategies suitable for qualified investors
