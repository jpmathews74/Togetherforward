Our Mission & Values

Guiding principles that shape every decision we make and every relationship we build

Our Mission

"To empower philanthropic organizations with exceptional investment stewardship that amplifies their mission impact, ensuring sustainable resources for meaningful change in communities nationwide."

At Together Forward Capital, we believe that every philanthropic dollar has the potential to create lasting positive change. Our mission extends beyond traditional investment management—we are dedicated partners in your organization's journey to maximize both financial returns and mission impact.

We understand that for foundations, endowments, and nonprofits, investment decisions carry unique responsibilities. Your assets represent more than numbers on a statement; they embody the hopes, dreams, and generous spirits of donors who trust your organization to create meaningful change.

Through disciplined investment strategies, transparent communication, and unwavering commitment to fiduciary excellence, we help ensure that your organization's financial foundation remains strong and sustainable, enabling you to focus on what matters most—fulfilling your mission and serving your community.

Our Core Values

These fundamental beliefs guide our approach to serving philanthropic organizations

🤝

Fiduciary Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of fiduciary responsibility, always acting in your organization's best interests with complete transparency, objectivity, and accountability in every decision we make.

🎗️

Mission Alignment

We recognize that your investments must reflect your values. Our strategies integrate your organization's mission and ethical guidelines, ensuring that your portfolio supports rather than conflicts with your purpose.

🌱

Sustainable Stewardship

We believe in long-term thinking that preserves and grows your assets across generations. Our disciplined approach balances current needs with future opportunities, ensuring perpetual impact.

💡

Educational Partnership

We empower your board and staff with knowledge and insights needed for informed decision-making. Through ongoing education and clear communication, we strengthen your organization's financial acumen.

🛡️

Risk Awareness

We maintain a thoughtful approach to risk management, understanding that preserving your assets is as important as growing them. Our strategies are designed to protect your organization's ability to serve its mission.

🔄

Continuous Innovation

We stay at the forefront of investment strategies and philanthropic best practices, continuously evolving our services to meet the changing needs of nonprofit organizations in an dynamic environment.

Our Commitment to You

When you partner with Together Forward Capital, you gain more than an investment advisor—you gain a dedicated ally committed to your organization's long-term success.

  • Personalized Attention: Every organization is unique, and we tailor our services to your specific needs, goals, and constraints.
  • Proactive Communication: We believe in transparency and keep you informed with regular reporting and timely market insights.
  • Collaborative Approach: We work closely with your team, board, and other advisors to ensure coordinated and effective financial management.
  • Ethical Standards: We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our dealings, recognizing the trust you place in us.
  • Long-term Partnership: We're here for the long haul, committed to growing alongside your organization through changing times and evolving needs.
