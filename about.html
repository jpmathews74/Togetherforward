Our Story

From vision to reality—how a commitment to philanthropic excellence shaped our journey

The Beginning

Together Forward Capital was born from a simple yet powerful observation: philanthropic organizations deserve investment partners who truly understand their unique mission and challenges. Joel Mathews, a seasoned investment professional with deep experience in both traditional wealth management and nonprofit advisory, recognized a significant gap in the marketplace.

While many investment firms offered generic solutions to institutional clients, few possessed the specialized knowledge and genuine passion required to serve foundations, endowments, and nonprofits effectively. These organizations needed more than just portfolio management—they needed partners who understood the delicate balance between preserving capital, generating sustainable returns, and maintaining alignment with their values and mission.

"Too often, there's a disconnect between how financial advisors operate and what today's donors and philanthropic organizations truly need. At Together Forward Capital, we bridge that gap by aligning investment expertise with purpose-driven outcomes. We're redefining how capital supports mission — with thoughtful strategy, transparency, and a deep commitment to impact." - Joel Mathews, Founder

A Personal Calling

The inspiration for Together Forward Capital came from Joel's personal experience serving on nonprofit boards and witnessing firsthand the challenges these organizations faced. Board meetings often centered around investment concerns rather than mission advancement. Trustees grappled with complex fiduciary responsibilities without adequate support. Investment strategies seemed disconnected from organizational values.

This disconnect troubled Joel, who believed deeply that an organization's investments should amplify, not complicate, its ability to create positive change. He envisioned a different kind of advisory relationship—one built on expertise, transparency, and genuine partnership in pursuit of philanthropic goals.

The Journey to Together Forward

Four key moments that shaped our commitment to philanthropic excellence

01

The Insight

Through years of investment experience and nonprofit board service, Joel recognized that philanthropic organizations needed advisors who truly understood their unique challenges—from UPMIFA compliance to mission alignment—rather than generic institutional solutions.

02

The Vision

In early 2024, Joel developed a comprehensive framework for specialized philanthropic investment advisory, focusing exclusively on foundations, endowments, and nonprofits who deserved partners as committed to their mission as they were.

03

The Launch

Together Forward Capital was established in 2025 as a specialized practice within R.F. Lafferty & Co., combining Joel's entrepreneurial vision with proven regulatory infrastructure and institutional-quality investment capabilities.

04

The Mission

Today, Joel is building relationships with forward-thinking philanthropic organizations who share his commitment to excellence, transparency, and the belief that investment strategy should amplify—not complicate—mission impact.

The Principles That Guide Us

Specialized Focus

Unlike generalist firms, we exclusively serve philanthropic organizations, developing deep expertise in the unique challenges and opportunities facing foundations, endowments, and nonprofits.

Fiduciary Excellence

We take our fiduciary responsibilities seriously, providing transparent guidance that always prioritizes our clients' best interests and long-term mission success.

Mission Alignment

Every investment strategy we develop reflects our clients' values and mission, ensuring that portfolios support rather than undermine organizational purpose.

Educational Partnership

We believe in empowering our clients through education, providing boards and staff with the knowledge needed to make confident, informed decisions.

Long-term Thinking

Our strategies are designed for sustainability and perpetuity, ensuring that today's decisions support tomorrow's philanthropic impact.

Authentic Service

We maintain the personal touch of a boutique firm while leveraging institutional-quality resources, ensuring every client receives the attention they deserve.

Together Forward Today

What began as a vision to better serve philanthropic organizations has evolved into a focused practice dedicated to partnering with foundations, endowments, and nonprofits across the nation. As a newly launched specialized practice, we're building relationships with forward-thinking organizations who share our commitment to excellence.

Today, we remain committed to the principles that guided our founding: putting our clients' missions first, providing transparent and objective guidance, and building long-term partnerships that amplify philanthropic impact.

Our story is just beginning, with each conversation, each consultation, and each partnership opportunity representing the foundation for meaningful relationships that will advance the cause of positive change in communities nationwide.
