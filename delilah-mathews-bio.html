Delilah C. Mathews brings over 25 years of operational excellence, compliance expertise, and strategic leadership to Together Forward Capital, where she serves as Partner, Chief Operating Officer, and Head of Client Relations. Her unique combination of advanced education, professional certifications, and extensive experience in complex regulatory environments positions her perfectly to build the firm's operational infrastructure from the ground up while ensuring exceptional service delivery to philanthropic partners.

Professional Background & Experience

Throughout her distinguished career, Delilah has demonstrated exceptional leadership in managing complex operations, ensuring regulatory compliance, and building strong stakeholder relationships. Her extensive experience includes over two decades of managing multifaceted projects, coordinating interdisciplinary teams, and maintaining the highest standards of professional excellence in highly regulated environments.

Delilah's background spans comprehensive work with data-driven decision-making, regulatory compliance, and strategic planning. She has successfully managed Florida and Illinois State Board of Education documentation requirements, Medicaid compliance protocols, and complex stakeholder communications throughout her career. This deep experience in navigating regulatory frameworks and maintaining meticulous documentation standards provides the perfect foundation for overseeing Together Forward Capital's SEC compliance and fiduciary obligations.

Leadership at Together Forward Capital

As Chief Operating Officer of this RIA, Delilah is architecting comprehensive operational systems designed specifically for philanthropic organizations. Her proven expertise in developing and implementing individualized service plans translates seamlessly into creating customized investment management processes that address each nonprofit client's unique mission and goals.

In her role as Head of Client Relations, Delilah is developing the firm's client service framework with an unwavering focus on personalized attention and mission alignment. Drawing on her extensive experience in stakeholder coordination and cross-functional team leadership, she is creating processes that will guide philanthropic partners from initial consultation through long-term partnership.

Areas of Expertise

  • Operational Excellence: Building scalable systems and processes that ensure efficient service delivery while maintaining the highest standards of quality and compliance
  • Regulatory Compliance: Deep understanding of complex regulatory environments, with proven ability to implement and maintain comprehensive compliance frameworks
  • Client Relations: Exceptional ability to build trust with diverse stakeholders, translating complex concepts into clear, actionable insights for informed decision-making
  • Data Management: Expertise in data-driven decision-making, performance tracking, and maintaining accurate records for regulatory and operational purposes
  • Technology Integration: Proficiency in Google Suite and various data management platforms, enabling implementation of cutting-edge reporting and communication systems
  • Team Leadership: Proven track record as a mentor and team leader, fostering cultures of excellence and continuous improvement

Educational Background & Professional Credentials

Delilah holds a Master of Education (M.Ed.) degree in Communication Disorders from North Carolina Central University, where she graduated in June 2000. She also earned a Bachelor of Arts degree with honors in Communication Sciences and Disorders from the University of Florida in May 1998.

Her professional certifications include:

  • Certificate of Clinical Competence in Speech-Language Pathology (CCC-SLP) from the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association (ASHA)
  • Florida Department of Financial and Professional Regulation licensure
  • PECS (Picture Exchange Communication System) Certification
  • Clinical Fellow Mentor designation (2022-2023)

Delilah's approach to operational leadership is rooted in her belief that exceptional service delivery requires both systematic precision and genuine human connection.
